Engineering Design and Innovation (EDI)

EDI projects encourage students to apply engineering principles to real-world problems. They foster creativity, teamwork, and hands-on experience. At VIT, EDI helps bridge classroom learning with innovation and entrepreneurship.

Design Thinking (DT)

DT projects emphasize problem-solving through empathy, ideation, and prototyping. At VIT, DT encourages students to understand user needs, think critically, and develop human-centered solutions that can have social and industrial impact.

Course Projects

Course projects integrate knowledge from academic subjects into practical applications. They strengthen technical depth, analytical skills, and subject mastery. VIT students benefit by applying classroom concepts in real-life scenarios and research.
